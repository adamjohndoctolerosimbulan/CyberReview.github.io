VALORANT is a global arena in which you can compete. It is a 5v5 tactical shooter in which players can take turns planting and deactivating the spike with a system of 1 life per LI round in a series of 13 rounds. What you'll need more than guns and bullets is choosing an Operator equipped with fast, lethal, and adaptable abilities to create the right opportunities for you to dazzle everyone with your superior gun skills.
